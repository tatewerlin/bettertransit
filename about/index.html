US public transit is caught in a downward spiral.

Decreasing ridership leads to reduced funding. Less funding creates adverse transit experiences. Better Transit seeks to break this cycle, making transit an enjoyable and rewarding experience and attracting ridership to break the spiral.

Better Transit exists to improve public transit by increasing ridership, offering accessible universal passes that create seamless commute and travel experiences.
